Resources

The Tulsa Life Chart data was collected through RedCap using custom made instrument. The diffrent epochs from Birth to the current age of the subject were separated within 9 intervals.

RedCap Instruments Downloads

  • Life Chart Interview A: Birth
  • Life Chart Interview B: Birth to Start of Elementary School
  • Life Chart Interview C: Elementary School Years
  • Life Chart Interview D: Middle School or Junior High Years
  • Life Chart Interview E: High School Years
  • Life Chart Interview F: Young Adult Years
  • Life Chart Interview G: Ages 25-35
  • Life Chart Interview H: Ages 35-45
  • Life Chart Interview I: Ages 45-55

Possible issues when importing to RedCap. We used a sql field type to reference the employee names for "Interview conducted by:" This code will need to be modified for the specific environment it is placed in.
